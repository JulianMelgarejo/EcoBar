About Us

Our product is a multilingual eco-friendly bar. Here, people can enjoy drinks made from sustainable ingredients while practicing languages. We use only sustainable products, like recycled cups and straws. This bar is a space where people can come together to learn and speak different languages in a fun and relaxed environment. It is a new way to mix social life and language learning.

Target Markets

Our target markets are young professionals, students, and tourists. These groups are often looking for places to meet new people and practice languages. Also, they are more likely to appreciate our focus on sustainability. By targeting these markets, we aim to create a community of language learners who are also committed to eco-friendly practices.

Locations

Our bar will be located in city centers and near universities where there are many young people and tourists. These areas are ideal because they have a high concentration of people interested in learning languages and being part of an eco-friendly community. By placing our bar in these locations, we make it easy for our target customers to find and visit us.

Prices

Our prices will be competitive with other bars, but we will offer discounts to students and groups. This strategy helps attract our target markets while ensuring that our prices are affordable. We believe that by keeping our prices fair, more people will be encouraged to visit and become regular customers, helping us grow our community.

Advertising Strategies

We will use social media to reach our target audience and promote our eco-friendly message. We will also partner with language schools and universities to offer special events and promotions. Additionally, we will host language exchange nights where people can practice speaking with native speakers for free. This strategy will help us attract language learners and build a loyal customer base.
